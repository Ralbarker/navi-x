Adding Repositories

Repositories allow you to extend your available options of applications you can install. 
By default, the "Boxee Official Repository" is installed on your system. 

The following steps can help you add a new repository.

   1. Open the App Box
   2. Choose "Repositories"
   3. Click on "Add Repository"
   4. Type the address of the repository
